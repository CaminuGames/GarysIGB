The Mission: Clear the Books. Grab the Loot.

Meet **Gary, a disgruntled corporate accountant**, and **Bill, his trusty sidekick penguin**. When a routine audit reveals massive cosmic embezzlement, Gary and Bill decide to cut out the middleman and grab the interplanetary riches themselves.

*Intergalactic Booty Grab* is a fast-paced, 2D action-platformer blending classic tight controls with ridiculous sci-fi gadgetry. Jump, slash, and calculate your way through derelict space stations, hostile alien payroll offices, and zero-gravity vaults to secure the biggest payday in the galaxy.
